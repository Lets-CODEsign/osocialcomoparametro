Bio
Carlos Eugênio Moreira de Sousa
Coordinator of LED (Laboratory of Digital Experience) at UFC 
Assistant Professor at Department of Architecture and Urbanism 

Eugênio Moreira has a bachelor's degree in Architecture and Urbanism (2011) and a master's degree in Architecture, Urbanism and Design (2018) in the research line Urban Planning and Information Design, both from the Federal University of Ceará (UFC). He is an Assistant-A professor at the same University (since 2019), where he teaches disciplines in the areas of Perception and Representation of Form and Architectural Design. He has experience in management and conservation of the built cultural heritage, having served as Articulator of the Material Heritage Cell of the Department of Historical and Cultural Heritage of the Municipal Office of Culture of Fortaleza (2013-2014). His academic interests include digital processes in Architecture, Urbanism, Product and Graphic Design (with emphasis on parametric design and digital fabrication), Building and City Information Modeling, and Architectural, Urbanistic and Landscape Heritage Documentation.
