Bio

He studied architecture in Vienna and Paris and received a M.Sc in architecture from the Technical University Vienna before completing his M.Arch II with distinction at London’s Architectural Association School of Architecture Graduate School Design Research Lab (AADRL).

After graduation he worked with Will Alsop, ocean_uk and Zaha Hadid Architects in London and Vienna and co- founded his design and research practice unsquare.org. He is co-founder and director of hoppe + partner architekten.

He has been teaching and lecturing in Austria and internationally, among others working as Assistant Professor with Patrik Schumacher at the Department for Experimental Architecture at the University of Innsbruck (AT) from 2005 to 2009, as Assistant Professor with Zaha Hadid at the Institute of Architecture at the University of Applied Arts in Vienna from 2007 to 2015 and as Assistant Professor with Kazuyo Sejima from 2015 to 2016. 2015 and 2016 he has been teaching the Post Professional Design Studio at PennDesign's AAD program at the University of Pennsylvania (US). He is currently holding the position of Lecturer at the Institute for Structure and Design at the University Innsbruck (AT).

Robert R. Neumayr is currently coordinating a research group on Agent Based Parametric Semiology and is a PhD candidate at the University of Applied Arts in Vienna.
