Bio
Yongje(Yong-je) Byeon is a master student, concentrating on Design Computation at the Mind Computation Lab, Inha University. He completed his B.Arch at Inha University, South Korea. He is participating in research projects focusing on an advanced agent-based simulation integrated with Generative Adversarial Networks (GAN). His main focus is on enhancing human-oriented design computational methods with generative AI technologies and their practical application on design praxis. He is interested in agent-based simulation, generative modeling, data collection, and server construction.
