Bio
Professor at Nanjing University, Nanjing, Jiangsu, China
He graduated from the Department of Architecture of Southeast University in 1994 and received his Ph.D. from ETH Zurich in 2007. From May 1994 to October 2000, he taught at Southeast University, and from December 2001 to the present, he has taught at Nanjing University, where he has been the director of the Computer-Aided Architectural Design Laboratory of the School of Architecture and Urban Planning and the assistant dean of the School of Architecture and Urban Planning. He is now dean of School of Architecture and Urban Planning.

He is mainly engaged in the research and practice of architectural design and its theory, computer-aided architectural design and digital architecture. He presided over 1 project of National Natural Science Foundation of China, participated in 1 key project of National Natural Science Foundation of China, published 6 monographs and textbooks, 38 papers, and won more than 10 design awards such as the First Prize of National Excellent Engineering Survey and Design Industry Award and the First Prize of Excellent Engineering Survey and Design of Ministry of Education. He has won more than 10 design awards including the National Excellent Engineering Survey and Design Industry Award and the Ministry of Education Excellent Engineering Survey and Design Award.  He is a member of the Ninth Council of the Civil Engineering and Architecture Society of Jiangsu Province, a member of the Architectural Creation Committee and the Urban Design Committee.
