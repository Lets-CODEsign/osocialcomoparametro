Bio
Master of the Graduate Program in Architecture, Urbanism and Design - PPGAUD-UFC. Substitute teacher at the Federal Institute of Education, Science and Technology of Ceará (IFCE), Quixadá campus. Post-graduate "lato sensu" in Architecture and Sustainable Project at the University of Fortaleza (UNIFOR). Bachelor in Architecture and Urbanism from the Federal University of Ceará (UFC), with exchange held at Escuela Tecnica Superior de Arquitectura (ESTA) at the University of Valladolid (UVA) in Valladolid, Spain during the month of September 2011 to July 2012. Architect with experience in project analysis and construction management, working with the company Somma Arquitetura during the VI Phase of the Expansion of Shopping Iguatemi Fortaleza. In addition, she is co-author of articles on the use of building information modeling (BIM) software in the construction industry.
