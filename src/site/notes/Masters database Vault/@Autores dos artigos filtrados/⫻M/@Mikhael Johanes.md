Bio
Mikhael Johanes
University of Indonesia | UI · Department of Architecture
S.Ars M.Ars
Currently a Ph.D. researcher at EPFL Media x Design Lab investigating machine learning of architectural space.

Mikhael Johanes is a junior lecturer and research assistant in Department of Architecture, Faculty of Engineering Universitas Indonesia since finished his graduate degree on Master of Architecture on the same department in 2013. His current works is primarily specialized on healthcare design and engineering research while also teaching on digital media class and architectural design studio. He is interested in computational design and digital media research in architectural design. More specifically his works examines the roles of computational medium on its generative and analytical possibility in architectural design while also put it as a part of design pedagogy in current architectural education.
