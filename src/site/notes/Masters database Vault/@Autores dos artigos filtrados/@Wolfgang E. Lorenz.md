Bio

Wolfgang E. Lorenz is a researcher and lecturer at the Vienna University of Technology, where he did his PhD on  "Fraktalähnliche Architektur - Einteilung und Messbarkeit" (fractal-like architecture) and his M.Arch. "Fractals and Fractal Architecture". He is now a lecture at the same university.
