Bio
Dr. Luigi Pintacuda (ARB RIBA PhD SFHEA) is a chartered architect and academic leader, currently serving as the Head of Architecture and Design at the University of Hertfordshire. His extensive experience spans managing, teaching, and conducting innovative research in higher education institutions globally. With a keen focus on urban-scale and architectural design, Luigi's work emphasises sustainable urban futures through a design-driven approach and the integration of GIS data. He has led numerous national and international research projects, collaborating with diverse stakeholders and contributing extensively to scholarly literature, including books, chapters, and peer-reviewed articles. His commitment to advancing architectural education and research is reflected in his active involvement in various professional and academic committees and networks.
