Bio
The Phd Research Programme at the Architectural Association

The Ecological Superblock Design Science for the Architecture of the City
Supervisors: Dr Michael Weinstock, Dr George Jeronimidis
The design research uses evolutionary computation to create novel morphologies and topologies of city tissues, and to develop a generative computation model applicable to different climates and ecological systems by modifying the operative and critical parameters. The project seeks to establish a methodology based on principles extracted from design science and new developments in the biological sciences, advances in material sciences and new modes of production, including digital fabrication.

_LlabresTabony Architects_ is a London based _multidisciplinary_ design studio specialized in the fields of architecture, urbanism, geography, ecology and landscape architecture. The ethos of the practice relies in its critical approach to ecology and its relationship with the built environment. Ecology is understood as an artificial construction which subject and object must be continuously revisited based on a relational construction of space time and value.

Their co-founders, Aiman Tabony and Enriqueta Llabres have a planetary commitment with the environmental challenges the humanity is facing today, stressing the need for cultural identity and the sense of place. Each project is conceived as a dynamic complex system, where the architecture of the building is an extension of the synergies and aesthetic values occurring during the building life across different scales.

The practice has developed an unique design methodology based on using critically the latest technology to read the environment, visualize the invisible and merge it with the context and the identity of the place. A tacit and intuitive engagement with technology that allows for the humanization of technique, a symbiosis between man and machine used to sculpt delicately the spaces while keeping the proportions and the equilibrium of the material distribution. Each project has a tailored design approach bringing expertise from Architecture to Ecology, Social Sciences, Engineering, Visual Arts and Programming.

Enriqueta Llabres and Aiman Tabony  founded LlabresTabony Architects in 2017, the London based design studio is built on the legacy brought by both partners after two decades of experience in the field. Enriqueta Llabres, co-founder of Relational Urbanism, has guided her professional career to explore the untapped potential between technology, culture and innovation. Her approach to space as relational has led her to explore the identity in the projects emerging from the active participation of living and non-living systems. She continues her research in Relational Urbanism under the umbrella of Relational Urbanism Lab. Aiman Tabony is a second generation architect in Dr Tabony Architects. An architecture and engineering office founded in Nazareth in the 1960s by his father the architect and engineer Dr. Mahmoud Tabony. Aiman, together with his brother the engineer Ashraf Tabony leads the studio since Mahmoud’s passing in 2005.
