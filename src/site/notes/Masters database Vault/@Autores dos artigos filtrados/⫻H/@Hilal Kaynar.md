Biography
Hilal Kaynar obtained her B.Sc. from Mimar Sinan Fine Arts University, Department of Architecture in 2013. During her undergraduate education, she studied as an exchange student at Vienna University of Technology. Following her undergraduate studies, she gained professional experience over a five-year period by working on architectural design and implementation projects of various scales. In the fall semester of 2018, she took place as a researcher at AURA Istanbul. She completed her master's degree in the Architectural Design program at Istanbul Technical University in 2023. In the same year, she began her doctoral studies in the Building Design Theory and Methodology program at Mimar Sinan Fine Arts University, which she is currently pursuing.
