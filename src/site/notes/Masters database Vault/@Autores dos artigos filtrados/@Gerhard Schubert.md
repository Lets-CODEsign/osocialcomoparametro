Bio
Gerhard Schubert is a Senior Researcher at the Chair of Architectural Informatics at the Technical University of Munich. He studied architecture at the Technical University of Munich, graduating with honours in 2006, and finished his doctoral dissertation in the field of Human Computer Interaction in 2014 with highest distinction. Since 2015, he also holds the position of Director of Research at the Faculty of Architecture.
