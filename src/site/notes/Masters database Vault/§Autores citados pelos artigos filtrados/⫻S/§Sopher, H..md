Hadas Sopher
I am a postdoc fellow at the AAU lab, France, the winner of the West Creative Industries grant. I obtained a PhD from the Faculty of Architecture and Town Planning, Technion, Israel. I am a qualified architect and experienced educator at a university level. As a teacher, I designed innovative syllabi that use immersive virtual environments to support learning. During my PhD studies, I won the Guthwirth and Jacobs scholarships and the Schragenheim prize for doctoral studies. My research interests are centred on architecture learning processes and virtual environments, design cognition and collaborative design.
