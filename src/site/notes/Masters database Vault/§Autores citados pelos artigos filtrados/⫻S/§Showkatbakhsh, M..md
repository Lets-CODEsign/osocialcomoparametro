Milad Showkatbakhsh is an accomplished architect, educator, and researcher specializing in computational design and emergent technologies. He is the Co-Director of the Emergent Technologies and Design AA (EmTech) program at the Architectural Association (AA) and the Co-Director of the AA DLAB and AA Istanbul Visiting School. Milad's work integrates biological intelligence principles into architecture, redefining adaptive environments using computational methodologies. He holds a Bachelor's degree in Architectural Engineering from Shahid Beheshti University and a Master's in Architecture from Pratt Institute, where he received the Sidney Katz Award in 2015. His PhD at the AA focused on evolutionary models for urban morphologies with homeostatic behaviours.
