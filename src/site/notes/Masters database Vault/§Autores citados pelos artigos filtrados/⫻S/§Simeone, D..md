Davide Simeone
In my research experiences in Italy and abroad - as an invited visiting scholar at Technion Israel Institute of Technology (2011-12) and University of Berkeley (2013)- and in my post-doc research I’ve been working on different topics such as behavioral simulation in buildings, BIM for architectural heritage, data and knowledge management in the construction industry.  
Currently, my major research interests fall within the scope of digital twins implementation for construction sites, predictive models for lean construction and 4d/5d modeling, and gamification for sustainability in the construction industry.
