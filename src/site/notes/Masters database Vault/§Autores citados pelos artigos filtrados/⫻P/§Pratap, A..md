Amrit Pratap was born in Hyderabad, India, on August 27, 1979. He received the M.S. degree in mathematics and scientific computing from the Indian Institute of Technology, Kanpur, India, in 2001. He is working toward the Ph.D. degree in computer science at the California Institute of Technology, Pasadena, CA.,He was a member of the Kanpur Genetic Algorithms Laboratory. He is currently a Member of the Caltech Learning Systems Group. His current research interests include evolutionary computation, machine learning, and neural networks.(Based on document published on 7 August 2002).
