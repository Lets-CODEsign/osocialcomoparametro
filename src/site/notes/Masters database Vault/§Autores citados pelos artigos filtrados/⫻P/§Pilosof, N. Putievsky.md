Dr. Nirit Putievsky Pilosof is an architect and researcher, a fellow of Cambridge Digital Innovation and an Associate of Cambridge Judge Business School at the University of Cambridge in the UK. Nirit holds a Ph.D. from the Technion - Israel Institute of Technology, a Post-Professional M.Arch from McGill University, and an EDAC from the Center for Health Design in the USA. She practised healthcare architecture as a design project manager at leading architecture firms and won international awards, including the prestigious AIA Academy of Architects for Health award, the American Hospital Association (AHA) graduate fellowship, and the Azrieli Foundation fellowship. Currently a Faculty Member at Tel Aviv University.
