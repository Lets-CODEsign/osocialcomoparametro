Ramon Elias Weber is an Assistant Professor of Architecture at the University of California, Berkeley. He earned a PhD in Building Technology from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology where he was a researcher with the Digital Structures Group and the Sustainable Design Lab. At Berkeley he is investigating how computational design methods and simulation tools can create more sustainable architecture.
