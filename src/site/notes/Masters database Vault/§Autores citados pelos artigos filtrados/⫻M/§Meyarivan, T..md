T. Meyarivan was born in Haldia, India, on November 23, 1977. He is working toward the M.S. degree in chemistry from Indian Institute of Technology, Kanpur, India.,He is a Member of the Kanpur Genetic Algorithms Laboratory. His current research interests include evolutionary computation and its applications to biology and various fields in chemistry.(Based on document published on 7 August 2002).
