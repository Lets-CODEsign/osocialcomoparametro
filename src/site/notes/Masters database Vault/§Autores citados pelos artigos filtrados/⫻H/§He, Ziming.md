Ziming He
Lead Designer
M.Arch
Profile
Ziming He joined ZHA in 2018. He works as a computational design
developer and researcher with ZH-Social Group. His research is
focused on developing intelligent design systems embedded in the
design process and implementing design feature evaluation with social
parameters based on an agent-based system.
Education
Ziming holds a master’s degree from UCL Bartlett School of
Architecture, and a bachelor’s degree from the School of Architecture
at China Central Academy of Fine Art. He has over 10 years’
experience in independent architectural practices, and over 5 years’
experience on computational design related programming. He has also
taught in UCL Bartlett as a technical tutor since 2019.
