Bio
Peter E. Hart is a mostly-retired AI researcher and technology leader. He received the B.E.E. degree from Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute, and the M.S. and Ph.D. degrees in electrical engineering from Stanford University. He is a Fellow of the IEEE, ACM and AAAI, and is also a member of the SRI International and RPI Halls of Fame. Contact him at peter@peterhart.net.(Based on document published on 4 May 2021). 

While at the SRI International Artificial Intelligence Center, Hart co-authored 20 papers, among them the initial exposition of the A* search algorithm and the variant of the Hough transform now widely used in computer vision for finding straight line segments in images. He also contributed to the development of Shakey the Robot.[6]
