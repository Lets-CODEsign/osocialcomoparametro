Seung Wan Hong is an Associate Professor of the Architecture department at Inha University, a private research university located in Incheon, South Korea. His research focus on Design Computation, Human Behaviour Simulation, Artificial Intelligence and New Media-Applied Archtiectural Design, Computational Creativity Models. PhD from University of California, Berkeley (2013). Postdoctoral Fellow at The Technion-Israel Institute of Technology.
