Bio
- Many years ago (mid 90s) I used a spatial programming package developed by Intergraph that mapped space relationships and areas to building footprints (might have been called Project Programmer, but not sure it was part of their architectural design suite that sat on their IGDS: interactive graphic design system): seems that your approach plugged into this type of package would be a real boon to architects...I'm sure much of the work of architects is amenable to analysis in terms of mathematical topology and network theory...but the work is yet to be done!
