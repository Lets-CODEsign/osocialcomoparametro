Nancy Al-Assaf, an Assistant Professor at the University of Jordan, earned her Ph.D. in Architecture from Texas A&M University in 2019, specializing in computational design and Building Information Modeling (BIM). With a Master's from the University of Jordan, she focuses on the aesthetics of architectural form. Her current research integrates design theories with advanced computational methods, advancing innovative design pedagogies.
