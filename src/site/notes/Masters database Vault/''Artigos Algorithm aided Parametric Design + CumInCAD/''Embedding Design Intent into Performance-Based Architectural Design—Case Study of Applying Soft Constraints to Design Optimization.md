  - In response, this study proposes a method integrating subjective design intents into performance-based design optimization using soft constraints.
  - Therefore, it is also critical for the computational design tools or design approaches to support the iterative and human-in-the-loop design process. In light of this, the pro- posed design optimization is envisioned as continuously providing feedback to the designer from the outset of the design process rather than offering specific and determined solutions
  - For indirect constraints, the constraint is embedded into the design evaluation stage, using methods such as penalty or award functions.
  - the application of soft constraints has the advantage of maintaining the population diversity during the optimization process
  - "To conclude, this study is aimed to further extend the application of performancebased design optimization in architectural design and demonstrates how subjective\rdesign intents can be incorporated into the optimization process by using soft constraints."
