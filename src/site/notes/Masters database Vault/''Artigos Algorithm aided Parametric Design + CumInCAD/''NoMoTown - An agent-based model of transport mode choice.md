Justificativa:
  - Our goal is to find an equilibrium between individual freedom in transport choice, financial effort required for maintaining the overall transport system and the health of the whole population.
  - In our work, we have developed an agent-based simulation which offers mitigation strategies and tries to propose realistic lines of action for shifting transport modes to more sustainable and healthy modes.
  - We have presented a model called NoMoTown which shifts (commuter) transport to more sustainable modes. The main result is that we should monitor health of individual citizens for determining transport choice,
